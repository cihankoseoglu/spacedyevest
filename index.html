She weeps with me
I don't have a lot of time left in this world Nothing we do, nothing we fight for matters anymore I can't find a way to hold myself together A heart, made of glass, shattered in motion Every song I write, everything is about a different woman Every woman who touched like silk, who left a crackle in the glass For every woman who got engaged and married For every woman who left eventually And for those who stayed to get their eventual heartbreak Those that let me go Those I let myself I have loved you all dearly And I did love you all Yet I see clearly, freed from the blindness I see your face, through the blankness I see you for what you are I can't stop myself from touching your face and holding your hand As I see another one in this world made of myself Left with the glass fragments Little pieces, still in there, hurting everytime you move Everytime you look, everytime you feel I see a woman that weeps with me I see a woman who doesn't even know, still blind And I want to hold your hand, take you above the clouds, Below the ground, to the light in the horizon, where I left myself I want to hold you and smell your hair I want to hold you and never let go I want to force you to listen to my sad songs I want you to feel every single one of them I want you to feel like I do Left with pieces of glass, yet freed from the lies I want you to open your eyes and see what I see See that I can be there with you See that there's more to this world before we leave More than the random encounters we always feel More than the jade blossom More than the white armies of hope And I see in my dreams The times we weep, you and me Together, holding you in my arms, in our bed As you cry your sorrows And I feel your wet tears As your pale skin goes red I wish you were real and not in my head What you are is just another idea I can't exercise I wish I could feel your wet tears I wish you were actually here, so I will be there, telling you always We have no more time left in this world But we will leave together
